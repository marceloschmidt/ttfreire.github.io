About the game.

Use Ben's alien powers and Rook's Proto-Tool weapons to take down the evil genius Dr. Psychobos in a gigantic Ben 10 adventure!

My work in this project.

This was one of the first games I worked on mobile devices. Our development process was still very rough and communication among team members was vital. Since I had little experience testing games on tablets, I used this project as a sandbox to explore possible bugs related to the platform, like using gestures in different ways and opening other apps with the game on screen transitions.
