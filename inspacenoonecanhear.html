About the game.

My work in this project.

During my first semester on a game development specialization course, I made this game using Construct 2 for GB Jam #3. The art style and lack of sound were choices I made to make sure my focus was completely on finishing my first game and put my game design knowledge into practice. I really enjoyed the result and the reviews were really positive.
